Chili

| Time: 4 hours | Yield: 12 bowls |

Description

This easy chili recipe cooks on the stovetop and is perfect served alongside Homemade Cornbread, buttered toast or Buttermilk Biscuits. Add in your favorite toppings like cheese and onions for the perfect meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 2.5 Tbs chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 14.5 oz crushed tomatoes (canned)
  • 19 oz kidney beans
  • 14.5 oz diced tomatoes (with juice)
  • 1.5 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup beer
  • 1 Tbs tomato paste
  • 1 Tbs brown sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Brown beef, onions, garlic and some of the chili powder
  2. Drain any fat
  3. Let simmer
  4. Add remaining ingredientsand simmer uncovered
